## Collection of Personal Information

This site may collect personal information such as name and email address when users contact us.
This information is used to respond to inquiries and requests from users.

## Analytics Tools

This site uses **Cloudflare Analytics** to understand site usage and provide better services.

### Information Collected

Cloudflare Analytics automatically collects the following information:

- Page views
- Visitor count
- Referrer information (which site users came from)
- Browser type and version
- Operating system
- Device type
- Geographic location (country/region level)
- Access date and time

### Purpose of Information Collection

The collected information is used for the following purposes:

- Analysis of site usage
- Content improvement
- Enhancement of user experience
- Site performance improvement

### Data Protection

Cloudflare Analytics is designed with privacy in mind and does not collect personally identifiable information.
For more details, please review **[Cloudflare's Privacy Policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/)**.

## Purpose of Personal Information Use

Collected personal information is used for the following purposes:

- Responding to user inquiries
- Analysis for service improvement
- Communication as necessary

## Disclosure to Third Parties

This site does not provide personal information to third parties except with user consent or as required by law.

However, the following service receives information for service improvement:

- **Cloudflare Analytics:** Anonymous access information is provided to Cloudflare Inc. for site analytics.

These services handle information in accordance with their respective privacy policies.

## Management of Personal Information

We properly manage users' personal information and take measures to prevent unauthorized access, loss, damage, alteration, and leakage.

## Changes to Privacy Policy

The contents of this privacy policy may be reviewed and improved as necessary.
The revised policy will be promptly posted on this site.
